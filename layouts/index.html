About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Modeling and Simulation program at the University of Central Florida. I am an alumni of Winthrop University with a degree in Digital Information Design with a concentration in Web Application Development and minors in Computer Science and Outdoor Leadership.

My research interests lie in finding applications of AR/VR to address the sustainable development goals set forth by the UN. My dream project would be to find a way to use AR to help improve forestry efforts.

I am simply on a goal to learn more about the world, myself, and everything in between. I have lots of interests, and I love talking about them. "Lore" is my word of the year, and I love learning new lore every chance I get.
