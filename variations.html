VARIETY

Poppins is available in 9 different weights from thin to black. Each weight also has an italics version.

The typeface supports Latin and Devanagari writing systems. Each font includes 1014 glyphs, including all of the unique conjunct forms necessary for typesetting Indian languages like Hindi, Marathi, Nepali, etc.

Just like the Latin, the Devanagari is based on pure geometry, particularly circles.

The Devanagari design is particularly new, and is the first ever Devanagari typeface with a range of weights in this genre.

The Devanagari script, composed of 47 primary characters including 14 vowels and 33 consonants, is the fourth most widely adopted writing system in the world, being used for over 120 languages.
