FSL Season 3 Kick-off: Live-Stream + Raffle this Sunday

November 27, 2020

The Farming Simulator League kicks off with its third season this weekend. The finale of the tournament is broadcasted live on the official GIANTS Software Twitch channel. Not only can you watch an all-around improved FSL with new teams, machines, power-ups and strategic possibilities, you also have the chance to win some prizes in our chat raffles.

Tune in and win on Sunday!

The live-stream will start at 10am (CET) on Sunday, November 29th and will see eight finalists fight for their share of the season price pool worth 250.000 Euros.

John Deere, Lindner, Krone, Bednar as well as astragon and mYinsanity are returning to the field. But, there are new challengers entering the league: Valtra, Manitou, PÖTTINGER, Aebi and Hörmann are joining the list of seeded teams.

All logged-in viewers can participate in our chat raffle. This is what you can win:

  • Premium Edition of Farming Simulator 19
  • Alpine Farming Expansion for Farming Simulator 19
  • Platinum Expansion for Farming Simulator 19

Battle with friends or join the league!

The improved Tournament Client is free to download - for every Farming Simulator fan and esports enthusiast. Based on Farming Simulator 19, the game mode requires teamwork to expertly harvest wheat, produce bales and quickly deliver them to a barn. A training mode for newcomers is included.

Just register on fsl.giants-software.com to get your download key! Over on the FSL website you also find more news and information.
