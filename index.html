Spoons to the rescue

Have you ever thought about using spoons to get rid of boredom? You should! Spoons are great for all kinds of fun crafts and activities. You can use spoons to play games, make music, or even make a new friend.

You can balance a spoon on your nose for a cool trick!

Fun Tip

Spoons are fun that you don’t have to leave the house for.

Odds are you have at least one in your house. Probably even more than one.

You have the perfect cure to boredom right in your kitchen! They are durable and rounded so you don’t have to worry about breaking them or getting hurt while you play.

Best of all, you can use them to eat a yummy snack when you are done playing!
